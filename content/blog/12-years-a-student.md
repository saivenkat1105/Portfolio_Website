*Crying and weeping,*  
*Shouting and kicking,*  
*I set foot in this place.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*The happiness that was to follow.*  

*Tears rolling down my cheek,*  
*Fears starting to freak my mind,*  
*I passed through the gate.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*The tears and fears would forever go.*  

*With many more faces,*  
*Raining tears,*  
*I entered the class.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*After every rain, there came a rainbow.*  

*Hoping for the day to end,*  
*To go home and never come back again,*  
*I took my place.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*I would be waiting for tomorrow.*  

*Sobbing beside me,*  
*Competing with my cries,*  
*I saw my neighbour.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*He would become my shadow.*  

*Greeting with a smile,*  
*Comforting us with her words,*  
*I saw a woman.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*She would become my hero.*  

*With the cries slowly subsiding,*  
*Names started exchanging,*  
*I began to talk.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*The talks would only grow.*  

*Running, jumping, singing and dancing,*  
*Fighting, playing, crying and laughing,*  
*I spent my time.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*This time was bound to go.*  

*With every ring of the bell,*  
*With every day coming to an end,*  
*I wanted more.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*One day, there wouldn't be a tomorrow.*  

*Bunking classes, pranking teachers,*  
*Stalking my crushes, writing love letters,*  
*I couldn't ask for more.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*The swiftness with which time would flow.*  

*Days turned to months,*  
*Months to years,*  
*I knew the end was near.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*Without this place, life would be so hollow.*  

*Crying and weeping,*  
*Hugging and kissing,*  
*I walked slowly towards the gate.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*This last walk would be so slow.*  

*With every breath I took,*  
*With every step I took,*  
*I was flooded with memories.*  
*Never did I know,*  
*Life can give you such a blow.*  

*Wiping down my tears,*  
*Fighting down my fears,*  
*I left this place.*  
*One thing I did know,*  
*Nothing could beat this helluva show.*
